<p>Ren'Py based short visual novell game
 </p>
<p>Step into a few quiet, defining days in the life of a university student in the early 2000s.
 </p>
<p>This is a short, story-driven visual novel that takes approximately <strong>1–2 hours to complete</strong>. The narrative is mostly <strong>linear</strong>, focusing on atmosphere and emotional storytelling, but includes <strong>optional choices and side interactions</strong> that reveal additional character moments and deeper context.
 </p>
<p>Set in a pre-social-media world, the story captures the subtle tension of everyday life &mdash; where friendships are fragile, emotions are unspoken, and small moments can feel unexpectedly significant.
 </p>
<p>You’ll encounter themes of <strong>love, emotional attachment, misunderstandings, and quiet disappointment</strong>.</p>

<p>Features:
 </p>
<ul> <li> Short visual novel (approx. 1–2 hours)  </li><li> Mostly linear narrative with optional content  </li><li> Character-driven storytelling  </li><li> Early 2000s nostalgic atmosphere  </li><li> Themes of friendship, love, and emotional conflict  </li></ul>
<p>A brief story about ordinary days &mdash; and the feelings that stay longer than they should.
</p>
